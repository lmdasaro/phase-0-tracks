Tiny, the charming burrito-shaped chihuahua

Tiny is an adorable 5 year old chihuahua. He was born in Texas, but has lived a happy life in California. He enjoys lazy days, hunting, and refreshing night walks.

He may be small, but he is fierce! He likes to hunt a variety of critters:
  • lizards
  • gophers
  • small birds
  • field mice

He may be a affectionately called a burrito dog, but he should never be confused with an actual burrito!

He eats about everything, but his favorite treats have a heirarchy.
  1. duck jerky
  2. chicken jerky
  3. human food
